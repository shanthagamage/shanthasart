Saman Alwis

I am Saman Alwis, an artist based in Melbourne, Australia, originally from Sri Lanka. My passion for art has been with me since childhood, and I express myself through various mediums like soft pastels, pastel pencils, acrylic paint, oil pastels, and charcoal. Along with my art, I also share my love for music and singing on YouTube. My work has been inspired by iconic music figures such as Mahinda Bandara and W.D. Amaradeva. I've had the privilege of creating comic art for Sri Lankan tabloid newspapers and producing stunning pieces featuring animals, nature, and portraits of celebrities like Taylor Swift, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Shane Warne. My creative journey continues to merge my love for both art and music, reflecting my unique vision and skills.
